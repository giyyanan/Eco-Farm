    VISION


    "Our vision is to improve the agriculture supply chain by connecting farmers to retailers"






    The only way to provide a higher margin to farmer and lower price to consumer is toeliminate middlemen. We want maximum share of every rupee spent by a consumer to be transferred to farmer directly, thereby by improving the sustainability of Indian Agriculture eco-system




    While we source the vegetable directly from farmers, we understand the importance consumers give for high quality and touch & feel of the vegetable produce. Hence we facilitate that by branding the F&V retail stores. These partner stores joined us to benefit Indian farmer. We believe our consumer will also join us in the mission to benefit our farmers, thereby improving our Indian agriculture economy
